Who We Are


We are Christians, desiring to let the Lord make His home in our heart, while we are in college.

"That Christ may make His home in your hearts through faith..." Ephesians 3:17a




We want to pursue the Lord Jesus with others who love Him, that we may be prepared for every good work.

"...pursue...with those who call on the Lord out of a pure heart" 2 Timothy 2:22

Our Faith

The Bible is the complete divine revelation inspired word-by-word by God through the Holy Spirit.

"All scripture is God breathed..." 2 Timothy 3:16

God is uniquely one, yet Triune, the Father, the Son, and the Spirit.

"For there is one God..." 1 Timothy 2:5

The Son of God, even God Himself, was incarnated to be a man by the name of Jesus Christ.

"...the Word was God." John 1:1

Christ died on the cross for our sins, shedding His blood for our redemption.

"In Him we have redemption through His blood..."
Ephesians 1:7

Christ resurrected from among the dead on the third day.

"...He was raised on the third day..." 1 Corinthians 15:4

Christ ascended to the right hand of God to be Lord of all.

"... Exalted at the right hand of God..." Acts 2:33

Whenever any person repents to God and believes in the Lord Jesus Christ, he is born again

"...He who believes...shall be saved..." Mark 16:16

Christ is coming again to receive His believers to Himself.

"... Come Lord Jesus!" Revelation 22:20
